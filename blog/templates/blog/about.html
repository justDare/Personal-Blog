About

DareToProgram was created to to bring together a community of coders which anyone with the will to learn can be a part of. Here we abolish any sense of entitlement and try to grow together as developers, and most importantly, as people.

What will I find at DareToProgram?

Content may vary...

I want to keep the topics of conversation pretty open here, however, the sole purpose of the content you find here will be to help you on your journey to becoming a better programmer and person.

You will undoubtabley find some code tutorials, some tips and tricks, advice on where to start, etc., but we want to talk about more than just tech here. Personal health, lifestyle, and broader topics are all fair game here as well, so take a look around, you never know what you might find useful.
